The Glass Bead Game

 

Become the master of the game.

The game is a board game, turn based, for 2 players, and 5 bead each one. The objective of the game is to own as much from the board as possible, using the beads to do so.

 

The game is based on the teachings of Hermann Hesse and The Glass Bead Game (Das Glasperlenspiel). A game where the combination of music, mathematics and the natural elements of the world are combined.

 

The Glass Bead Game is "a kind of synthesis of human learning" in which themes, such as music, philosophical thought, and mathematics are stated.

- Wikipedia
